# 🧫 Microbial Genomics and Biodegradation (KU‑MGB)

### 🔍 About Us
We are a research group at the University of Copenhagen exploring microbial genomics, enzyme discovery, and AI‑driven biodegradation of PFAS and other persistent pollutants.

### 🧠 Research Focus
- PFAS‑degrading enzymes and biocatalysts  
- AI‑based protein structure prediction and molecular simulation  
- Environmental biotechnology and sustainable remediation
